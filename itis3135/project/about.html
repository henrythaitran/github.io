About VSA

VSA is a cultural, educational, and charitable organization at UNC Charlotte.

VSA Mission

Our mission is to promote Vietnamese culture and encourage higher education at UNC Charlotte.

What VSA Does

We spread awareness of the Vietnamese culture by hosting events, collaborating with other organizations, creating a positive environment promoting friendship, participating in charity events, traveling to support other VSAs all around, and many more!

Why VSA Matters

VSA recognizes the value of comprehending fresh perspectives on the Vietnamese American experience.
